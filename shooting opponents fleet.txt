Shooting opponents fleet

Actors: Two players
Precondition: Both players have arranged their fleets

1. Randomly chosen player starts the game
2. player A checks evidence grid and selects a target square to shoot
3. player a asks payer B for a square he is shooting
4. player B checks his fleet and replies with:
	a) "missed" if no ship is on the square
	b) "hit" if one of the ships contains the square
	c) "sunk" if hit square is the last square of a ship
5. players shift roles: player B shoots player A's fleet
6. Steps 2 - 5 are repeated until all ships from one player have been sunk.
7. The winner is the one who has at least one ship not sunk.
